# Data Preparation for Visualization

## Data Review
The dataset was explored to understand its structure, fields, and distributions. 
Columns include product placement, pricing, competitor pricing, promotion status, customer segment, category, and units sold.

## Data Validation
The dataset was checked for missing values, incorrect data types, and inconsistencies. No major issues were found.

## Data Structuring
Fields were reviewed to ensure they were suitable for Tableau analysis. Numeric fields such as price and units sold were validated.

## Field Formatting
Column names were standardized for clarity during visualization.

## Conclusion
The dataset was confirmed to be clean, structured, and ready for visualization in Tableau.
